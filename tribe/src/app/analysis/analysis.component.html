Ride Duration By Passholder Type

We want to analyze the difference of each passholder type. We grouped by passholder type and month to get the average riding duration time. This analysis also allows users to be able to see whether specific passholder type affects the trip duration.

People who hold monthly pass or walk-up tend to ride the bike for between 10 - 20 minutes on average and people who hold one-day pass tend to ride their bikes between 40 - 90 minutes
